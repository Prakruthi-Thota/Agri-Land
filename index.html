About Our Property

Our 3.24 acres of property basically yields good crop at all seasons. We have planted about 200+ cocunut tress, 300+ banana trees, 15+ palm trees and about an acre of CO4 grass for cattle. Our Fields

The area of about 3.24 acres has been equipped with good water source throughout the year for growing all types of crops.
